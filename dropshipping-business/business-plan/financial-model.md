## 3. Unit Economics Deep Dive

### Per-Order Breakdown

```
Revenue per order:                    $35.00   (100.0%)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Product cost:                         -$8.00   ( 22.9%)
Supplier shipping:                    -$3.00   (  8.6%)
Shopify txn fee (2.9% + $0.30):       -$1.32   (  3.8%)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Gross profit per order:               $22.68   ( 64.8%)

Customer acquisition cost (ads):     -$10.00   ( 28.6%)
Refund reserve (6%):                  -$2.10   (  6.0%)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Net profit per order:                 $10.58   ( 30.2%)
```

### Key Ratios

| Metric | Target | Healthy Range |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Gross margin | 64.8% | 55%–75% |
| Net margin (after ads) | 30.2% | 20%–40% |
| Customer acquisition cost | $10.00 | $5–$15 |
| Return on ad spend (ROAS) | 3.5x | 2.5x–5x |
| Average order value | $35.00 | $25–$50 |
| Lifetime value (LTV) estimate | $52.50 | Assumes 1.5 orders/customer |
| LTV:CAC ratio | 5.25:1 | >3:1 is healthy |

### Sensitivity Analysis

How net profit per order changes with different CPA and product cost combinations:

| | CPA $7 | CPA $10 | CPA $13 | CPA $16 |
|------------|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| **COGS $6** | $14.58 | $11.58 | $8.58 | $5.58 |
| **COGS $8** | $12.58 | $10.58 | $7.58 | $4.58 |
| **COGS $10** | $10.58 | $8.58 | $5.58 | $2.58 |
| **COGS $12** | $8.58 | $6.58 | $3.58 | $0.58 |

**Danger zone:** COGS above $12 combined with CPA above $13 makes the business unsustainable at a $35 retail price. Either raise prices or find cheaper suppliers.

## 5. Scaling Milestones

| Milestone | Trigger | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| First profitable month | Net profit > $0 | Continue current strategy, don't change what works |
| 100 orders/month | Consistent demand | Graduate top products to US-warehouse supplier for faster shipping |
| $5,000/mo revenue | Stable growth | Hire part-time VA ($400–$600/mo) for customer service |
| 200 orders/month | Scaling ads | Upgrade Shopify to $105/mo plan for better reporting + lower txn fees |
| $10,000/mo revenue | Product-market fit | Expand catalog by 50%, test adjacent sub-niches |
| $20,000/mo revenue | Strong brand | Consider holding inventory for top 3 products (better margins, 2-day shipping) |
| $50,000/mo revenue | Scaling business | Hire full-time operations person, explore wholesale/retail partnerships |

## 6. Tax & Legal Considerations

- Register an LLC or sole proprietorship before generating revenue (cost: $50–$500 depending on state)
- Obtain a sales tax permit and collect sales tax (Shopify handles calculation; use TaxJar or Shopify Tax for filing)
- Track all expenses for Schedule C (sole prop) or business tax return
- Set aside 25–30% of net profit for estimated quarterly taxes
- Consult a CPA when monthly revenue exceeds $5,000 — the cost ($200–$500/quarter) is worth the tax optimization
