# Pre-Launch Checklist

Complete every item before turning on paid advertising. Items marked **(critical)** must be done — the rest are strongly recommended.

---

## Legal & Business Setup

- [ ] **(critical)** Register business entity (LLC or sole proprietorship)
- [ ] **(critical)** Obtain EIN (Employer Identification Number) from IRS — free at irs.gov
- [ ] **(critical)** Open a dedicated business bank account (separate from personal)
- [ ] Register for sales tax permit in your state
- [ ] Set up accounting software (Wave — free, or QuickBooks)
- [ ] Purchase basic business insurance (optional but recommended: $20-50/mo)

---

## Shopify Store Configuration

### General Settings

- [ ] **(critical)** Store name and legal business name entered
- [ ] **(critical)** Business address configured (used for invoices, tax calculation)
- [ ] **(critical)** Store currency set to USD
- [ ] **(critical)** Time zone set correctly
- [ ] Contact email verified and monitored
- [ ] Customer accounts set to "optional" (don't require accounts to checkout)

### Domain & Email

- [ ] **(critical)** Custom domain purchased and connected (.com preferred)
- [ ] **(critical)** SSL certificate active (automatic with Shopify)
- [ ] Domain email set up (e.g., hello@yourstore.com via Google Workspace or Zoho)
- [ ] Redirect www to non-www (or vice versa) — configured in domain settings

### Payments

- [ ] **(critical)** Shopify Payments enabled and bank account connected
- [ ] **(critical)** PayPal configured as secondary payment option
- [ ] Test a real transaction end-to-end (place order, refund yourself)
- [ ] Enable Apple Pay and Google Pay in Shopify Payments settings
- [ ] Review and understand Shopify's chargeback process

### Shipping

- [ ] **(critical)** Shipping zones configured (US primary, international if applicable)
- [ ] **(critical)** Shipping rates set:
  - Free shipping on orders over $49 (or your threshold)
  - Standard shipping rate for smaller orders ($4.99-$6.99)
- [ ] **(critical)** Processing time set (3-5 business days recommended)
- [ ] Delivery time clearly stated on product pages and shipping policy page
- [ ] International shipping disabled initially (simplify operations)

### Taxes

- [ ] **(critical)** Shopify Tax or manual tax rates configured
- [ ] Tax-inclusive or tax-exclusive pricing decided (tax-exclusive is standard in US)
- [ ] Sales tax collection enabled for states where you have nexus

---

## Store Content & Pages

### Required Pages

- [ ] **(critical)** Homepage — hero banner, featured products, trust badges, testimonials
- [ ] **(critical)** About Us — brand story, mission, why customers should trust you
- [ ] **(critical)** Contact Us — email form, business email, response time expectation
- [ ] **(critical)** Shipping Policy — processing time, delivery estimates, international info
- [ ] **(critical)** Return & Refund Policy — 30-day guarantee, process, exceptions
- [ ] **(critical)** Privacy Policy — auto-generate via Shopify (Settings > Policies)
- [ ] **(critical)** Terms of Service — auto-generate via Shopify (Settings > Policies)
- [ ] FAQ page — address top 5-10 questions (shipping time, returns, quality)
- [ ] Track Your Order page — link to tracking service or AfterShip page

### Navigation

- [ ] **(critical)** Main menu: Home, Shop/Collections, About, Contact
- [ ] **(critical)** Footer menu: Shipping Policy, Returns, Privacy, Terms, FAQ, Contact
- [ ] Collection pages created and populated (Best Sellers, New Arrivals, categories)
- [ ] Search functionality works correctly

---

## Product Pages

### Content Quality

- [ ] **(critical)** Every product has a unique, compelling title (not copy-pasted from supplier)
- [ ] **(critical)** Every product has a detailed description (benefits over features)
- [ ] **(critical)** Pricing set using the pricing calculator (verify margins)
- [ ] **(critical)** At least 4-5 high-quality product images per product
- [ ] Compare-at price set for products on "sale" (shows crossed-out original price)
- [ ] Product type and vendor fields filled in (helps with filtering/reporting)
- [ ] Tags added for search and filtering
- [ ] Variant options properly configured (size, color, quantity)

### SEO

- [ ] **(critical)** Every product has a unique SEO title (under 60 characters)
- [ ] **(critical)** Every product has a unique meta description (under 160 characters)
- [ ] **(critical)** Image alt text filled in for all product images
- [ ] URL handles are clean and keyword-rich (e.g., /products/portable-massage-gun)
- [ ] No duplicate content across product pages

### Conversion Elements

- [ ] Trust badges visible below Add to Cart button
- [ ] Shipping estimate shown on product page ("Ships in 3-5 business days")
- [ ] Related products / "You may also like" section active
- [ ] Reviews widget visible and functional
- [ ] Mobile product page tested and functional

---

## Technical & Tracking

### Analytics

- [ ] **(critical)** Google Analytics 4 installed and receiving data
- [ ] **(critical)** Meta (Facebook) Pixel installed and tracking events
- [ ] **(critical)** Shopify Analytics accessible (built-in)
- [ ] Google Search Console connected and sitemap submitted
- [ ] TikTok Pixel installed (if running TikTok ads)
- [ ] Conversion events verified: PageView, ViewContent, AddToCart, Purchase

### Performance

- [ ] PageSpeed Insights score above 70 on mobile (test at pagespeed.web.dev)
- [ ] All images compressed and optimized (Shopify handles this mostly)
- [ ] No broken links (test with Shopify's built-in link checker)
- [ ] 404 page customized with search and link to homepage

### Security

- [ ] **(critical)** SSL certificate active (padlock icon in browser)
- [ ] Fraud analysis enabled in Shopify settings
- [ ] Staff accounts use strong passwords with 2FA
- [ ] Customer data handling complies with privacy policy

---

## Marketing Setup

### Email Marketing

- [ ] **(critical)** Klaviyo connected and syncing with Shopify
- [ ] **(critical)** Welcome email flow active
- [ ] **(critical)** Abandoned cart email flow active
- [ ] Post-purchase email flow active
- [ ] Email signup popup configured (appears after 5 seconds)
- [ ] Test all email flows by triggering them yourself

### Social Media

- [ ] **(critical)** Instagram business account created and linked
- [ ] **(critical)** TikTok account created
- [ ] Facebook Business Page created
- [ ] Pinterest business account created (optional but valuable)
- [ ] Social media links added to store footer
- [ ] At least 9-12 posts ready for launch week

### Advertising

- [ ] Meta Business Manager set up with ad account
- [ ] Meta Pixel verified and firing correctly
- [ ] First 3-5 ad creatives prepared (mix of video and static)
- [ ] Target audiences defined in Meta Ads Manager
- [ ] Daily ad budget set ($10-20/day to start)
- [ ] Google Merchant Center approved and product feed active

---

## Customer Experience

### Test the Full Customer Journey

- [ ] **(critical)** Browse store on mobile — does everything look right?
- [ ] **(critical)** Browse store on desktop — does everything look right?
- [ ] **(critical)** Add product to cart → checkout → complete purchase (test transaction)
- [ ] **(critical)** Verify order appears in Shopify admin
- [ ] **(critical)** Verify order syncs to DSers/supplier app
- [ ] **(critical)** Verify customer receives order confirmation email
- [ ] Test abandoned cart flow (add to cart, leave, check for email)
- [ ] Test contact form submission
- [ ] Test search functionality
- [ ] Review all pages on mobile for layout issues

### Pre-Written Responses

- [ ] Draft response templates for common inquiries:
  - "Where is my order?"
  - "How do I return this?"
  - "Product arrived damaged"
  - "When will this be back in stock?"
  - "Do you ship internationally?"

---

## Launch Day

- [ ] Remove any remaining "Coming Soon" or placeholder content
- [ ] Remove password protection from store (Settings > Preferences)
- [ ] Double-check all prices are correct
- [ ] Verify inventory is set correctly (or set to "Continue selling when out of stock" for dropshipping)
- [ ] Announce on social media
- [ ] Send launch email to any pre-collected email list
- [ ] Turn on first ad campaigns (start small: $10-15/day)
- [ ] Monitor orders, analytics, and customer inquiries closely for first 48 hours

---

## Post-Launch (First Week)

- [ ] Check analytics daily: traffic sources, conversion rate, bounce rate
- [ ] Monitor ad performance: CPA, CTR, ROAS
- [ ] Respond to all customer inquiries within 12 hours
- [ ] Process and fulfill all orders within 24 hours
- [ ] Document any issues and create SOPs for recurring tasks
- [ ] Adjust ad targeting based on first 3-5 days of data
- [ ] Post daily on TikTok and Instagram
