# Content Removal Guide for Unauthorized Webcam Recordings

A complete, step-by-step guide for removing unauthorized recordings and images from the internet. Designed for webcam performers whose live streams were recorded and reposted without permission.

## Quick-Start Summary

1. **Search everywhere** for your content and record every URL in the tracking spreadsheet
2. **Gather proof** of ownership (platform account screenshots, payout history)
3. **File DMCA takedowns** with each hosting site using the template
4. **File for de-indexing** with Google, Bing, and Yandex
5. **Report via NCII channels** on platforms that support it (and register with StopNCII.org)
6. **Escalate** if sites don't respond within 7-14 days
7. **Set up monitoring** to catch re-uploads

---

## Key Facts

- **You own the copyright** to your live performances automatically (Berne Convention)
- **Russian citizenship does not prevent** you from filing DMCA takedowns with US-based sites
- **Most compliant platforms remove content within 24-72 hours**
- **100% removal is extremely difficult**, but removing from major platforms and search engines eliminates most discoverability
- **Using a representative** (agent or lawyer) can protect your real identity during the process
