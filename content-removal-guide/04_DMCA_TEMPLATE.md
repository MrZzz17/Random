# DMCA Takedown Notice Template

Below is a ready-to-use DMCA takedown notice. Replace all `[BRACKETED]` fields with your information. Send this via email to the site's DMCA / abuse contact, or paste it into their web-based reporting form.

---

## Standard DMCA Takedown Notice

```
Subject: DMCA Takedown Notice — Unauthorized Use of Copyrighted Content

To Whom It May Concern,

I am writing to notify you of copyright-infringing material hosted on your
platform. This letter is a formal notification under the Digital Millennium
Copyright Act (DMCA), 17 U.S.C. § 512(c), and I request that you remove or
disable access to the infringing material described below.

IDENTIFICATION OF THE COPYRIGHTED WORK:

I am the original performer and sole copyright holder of the audiovisual
content described below. The content consists of live webcam performances
that were originally broadcast on [PLATFORM NAME, e.g., Chaturbate] under
the username "[YOUR STAGE NAME]". These performances were recorded without
my authorization and reposted on your platform.

IDENTIFICATION OF THE INFRINGING MATERIAL:

The following URL(s) contain unauthorized copies of my copyrighted content.
I request that each be removed or disabled immediately:

1. [FULL URL OF INFRINGING CONTENT #1]
2. [FULL URL OF INFRINGING CONTENT #2]
3. [FULL URL OF INFRINGING CONTENT #3]
[Add additional URLs as needed]

CONTACT INFORMATION:

Name: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
Address: [YOUR MAILING ADDRESS]
Email: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]
Phone: [YOUR PHONE NUMBER (optional)]

STATEMENTS:

I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material
described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent,
or the law.

I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this
notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am
authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner of an exclusive
right that is allegedly infringed.

SIGNATURE:

[YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
[DATE]
```

## Appendix A: Authorization Letter

Use this if a **representative** (lawyer, agent, or service) is filing on your behalf.

```
AUTHORIZATION TO FILE DMCA TAKEDOWN NOTICES

I, [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME], hereby authorize [REPRESENTATIVE'S FULL NAME
OR COMPANY NAME] to act as my designated agent for the purpose of filing
DMCA takedown notices and content removal requests on my behalf.

This authorization covers the removal of unauthorized recordings of my
live webcam performances originally broadcast on [PLATFORM NAME(S)]
under the username(s) "[YOUR STAGE NAME(S)]".

[REPRESENTATIVE NAME] is authorized to:
- Identify and document infringing content
- Send DMCA takedown notices to hosting platforms
- File content removal requests with search engines
- Communicate with platform representatives regarding my content
- Provide necessary identification on my behalf

This authorization is effective as of [DATE] and remains in effect until
revoked in writing.

Copyright Owner:
Name: [YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
Signature: ________________________
Date: [DATE]

Authorized Representative:
Name: [REPRESENTATIVE'S NAME]
Company: [COMPANY NAME, if applicable]
Email: [REPRESENTATIVE'S EMAIL]
```

## Appendix B: Follow-Up Template (if no response after 7 days)

```
Subject: Follow-Up: DMCA Takedown Notice — [YOUR STAGE NAME] — Sent [ORIGINAL DATE]

To Whom It May Concern,

I am following up on my DMCA takedown notice sent on [ORIGINAL DATE]
regarding unauthorized copies of my copyrighted content hosted on your
platform. I have not yet received confirmation of removal.

The infringing URLs previously reported are:
1. [URL]
2. [URL]

Under the DMCA, service providers must act expeditiously to remove
infringing material upon receiving a valid takedown notice to maintain
safe harbor protection under 17 U.S.C. § 512(c).

If the content is not removed within [3/5/7] business days, I will
escalate this matter to your upstream hosting provider, relevant CDN
services, and applicable search engines for de-indexing.

Please confirm receipt of this notice and the expected timeline for
removal.

Sincerely,
[YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]
[YOUR EMAIL]
[DATE]
```

## Tips for Sending

1. **Always use the site's official DMCA process first** (web form or designated email)
2. **Send from a professional email address** — avoid throwaway addresses as they may be ignored
3. **Keep records** — BCC yourself, save sent emails, screenshot web form confirmations
4. **Batch by site** — send one notice per platform with all infringing URLs for that site
5. **Be factual and professional** — emotional language reduces effectiveness
6. **Track everything** in the spreadsheet (`02_TRACKING_SPREADSHEET.csv`)
